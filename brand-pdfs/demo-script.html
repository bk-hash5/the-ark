Lightning Labs Demo Script — 3 Minutes
March 18, 2026 | arknode.ai | Pay-per-task AI on Lightning

Opening — The Problem (0:00–0:30)

"2 billion people in Africa and the Global South are being locked out of the AI revolution. Why? Three barriers: subscription paywalls that require international credit cards, mandatory identity verification that endangers journalists and activists, and data harvesting business models. We built The Ark to fix all three — using Bitcoin Lightning."
Pause briefly here. Let the problem land. This audience understands Lightning — they'll immediately see where you're going.

The Solution — Live Demo (0:30–1:45)

"The Ark is a privacy-first AI platform with 120+ services — all paid via Lightning. No accounts. No emails. No data collected. Let me show you."
Open arknode.ai on your phone/laptop. Show the clean chat interface.
"This is it. No login screen. No signup. You just type and go."
Type a prompt: "Write a 3-paragraph pitch for a Bitcoin startup targeting African markets" — tap Send.
"A Lightning invoice appears — 10 sats. That's $0.007. Not $20 a month. Not even $1. Ten sats."
Pay the invoice from your wallet (have Phoenix/Wallet of Satoshi ready). Show the response streaming in real-time.
"Payment confirmed, response streams instantly. The entire transaction — invoice generation, payment verification, AI execution — happens in under 2 seconds. That's Phoenixd + LNbits under the hood."
If the demo gods are kind, this takes 5 seconds. If payment is slow, keep talking about the architecture while it processes.

Chama — Community AI Access (1:45–2:15)

"Now here's what makes this uniquely African. In East Africa, we have a concept called Chama — community savings groups where people pool resources together. We brought that to AI."
Navigate to arknode.ai/groups — show the Chama interface.
"A group of 5 students can each contribute 10 sats and get shared AI access for research, essays, and study guides. Dev teams pool credits for code review. NGOs share translation and document creation. It's community-powered AI — and it all runs on Lightning group wallets."

Technical Edge & L402 (2:15–2:40)

"For the builders in the room: we support L402 — the machine-to-machine payment protocol. Any AI agent with a Lightning wallet can discover our services via our OpenAPI spec and pay programmatically. No API keys. No OAuth. Just HTTP 402 + a Lightning invoice. We also have workflow bundles — chain multiple services into a single atomic payment. Full code audit: review + security scan + tests for 1,200 sats."

Close — The Ask (2:40–3:00)

"The Ark is live today at arknode.ai. 120+ services. 5 free prompts for everyone. Built on Phoenixd, LNbits, and FastAPI. We're looking for integration partnerships with Lightning wallets and developer tools. Every person who uses Lightning can now access AI without giving up their identity or their data. Thank you."
End with confidence. Don't rush the last line. Let "without giving up their identity or their data" land.
